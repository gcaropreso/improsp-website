What moves us

this is theater

We make theater.

✕ Not group dynamics, icebreakers or stand-up. ✕ We don't sell corporate tools, productivity or personal development.

IMPRO.SP is an independent cultural institution. A school, an artistic collective and a research laboratory. We train artists, develop stage language and build community.

Here, improvisation means instant dramaturgy, listening, presence and collective creation. A stage with no safety net.

Figura Workshop

Visual stimuli for improvisation · with Gabriel Caropreso

We use images — photos, paintings and comics — to build scenes and create stage imagery. The format comes from the show #sigodevolta and has been to Thessaloniki, Barcelona and Cologne. This is its first time in São Paulo.

On the last day, the group takes the stage for an open showcase. The starting point: photos from the social media of whoever is in the audience.

The ecosystem

rehearsal continues on the sidewalk

Beyond the rehearsal room.

IMPRO.SP has no fixed venue because it occupies the city. Our work happens in theaters and partner spaces.

But the school is also a meeting point. Our calendar mixes shows, concerts, jams, parties, samba circles and bar gatherings.

We create scenes by building community.
