Figura Workshop

Visual stimuli for improvisation · with Gabriel Caropreso

Practical classes. We use still images — photos, drawings, figurative and abstract paintings — as triggers for the scene.

The work focuses on extracting characters, settings and narratives from visual detail, without depending on text or verbal themes. We investigate the creation of dreamlike stage imagery and the interaction with the image as space. Inspired by photo-novels and comics.

The final showcase. The process closes with a public performance of the FIGURA format: scenic improvisation built from photos taken from the social media of the audience present.

Audience capacity and accessibility conditions for the final showcase: being confirmed with the venue.

Sonora Workshop

Improvisation in the dark · with Gabriel Caropreso

An entirely practical experience held in total blackout. We investigate the creation of scenes, environments and characters using only sound: vocal expression, body percussion, noise and live music.

Focus on absolute listening and imaginative precision without visual references. In dialogue with narrative-podcast structures and collective listening games.

The final showcase. A public performance driven entirely by sound. Cast and audience share the same space in absolute darkness.
