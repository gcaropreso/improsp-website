01

The alliance

coffee & cigarettes collective

Theater made of truth, will and sweat.

IMPRO.SP does not research alone. Our base rests on circulation and on the ongoing partnership with Impro Synthesis, in Athens.

From this alliance was born the Coffee & Cigarettes Collective, an international theater company co-founded by Gabriel Caropreso (São Paulo) and Maro Lesioti (Athens). The collective grew out of their artistic encounter at the Irreverente Festival, in Lisbon.

We develop spontaneous dramaturgy through a bicontinental methodology. Parallel rehearsals in Athens and São Paulo, shared authorship, and a working practice that treats distance not as an obstacle but as raw material. It's theater made with urgency, risk and identity.

Where we've been

stops on the map

Our language is built on the road, in theaters, festivals and partnerships with groups that investigate risk around the world. Where our research has already set foot:

Lisbon

Portugal · 2025

Took part in the Irreverente International Improvisation Festival. We taught the SONORA workshop and premiered the show O Que Deixamos para Trás (What We Leave Behind), created and directed by Gabriel Caropreso.

Uppsala

Sweden

Performance of Eagerly Waiting on the stage of SWIMP — Sweden International Improv Festival.

Barcelona

Spain

The SONORA workshop, in direct partnership with TBC Improv Spain.

Cologne

Germany

Touring and language exchange in partnership with Impro Köln.

Thessaloniki

Greece · 2026

We brought our research to the TiVi Improv Festival, invited by the school Thess improVice (TiVi).

2026 Tour

the road goes on

The risk of the stage is the same on any sidewalk.

The exchange takes shape in the 2026 Tour. Our alliance with Impro Synthesis keeps the flow open for new cast crossings, joint laboratories and the circulation of stage formats created between São Paulo and Athens.

The research we do here feeds the rehearsal rooms there.
