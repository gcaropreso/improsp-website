01

Origin

respect for those who came before

A school in the center of the city.

IMPRO.SP was born to add to the São Paulo scene. We respect the history, the schools and the teachers who paved the way for improv theater here.

Our proposal is to open one more working space. A meeting point focused on continued research and on building an active community of artist-improvisers.

We treat the stage as a place of risk, error and live composition. We didn't come to teach quickness tricks or fast-entertainment techniques. We came to make contemporary theater. Collective, urban, attentive to the present.

The faculty

who leads the rehearsal

Four trajectories, one body of work. Pedagogical coordination and the São Paulo activities are led by Gabriel and Tamara, who live in the city.

Gabriel Caropreso

Actor · clown · improviser · musician · director — São Paulo

Investigates physical theater and clowning. Founding member of the collective Quase Todos / Quem Sobrou (ProAC 2018), where he co-directed the show SigoDeVolta. Took part in improvised-dramaturgy research and performed in street theater and performance pieces. Taught at Espaço da Comédia, Teatro dos Arcos and Improclube. In 2025, directed and taught at the Irreverente International Improvisation Festival in Lisbon.

Tamara Borges

Actress · theater teacher · cultural producer — São Paulo

Trained at Teatro Escola Célia Helena, holds a degree in Theater Arts from UNESP and a specialization in Cultural Management from CPF Sesc. Researches improvisation and clowning since 2010. Performed with Cia do Quintal (Jogando no Quintal) and at festivals in Sintra (Portugal) and Medellín (Colombia). Taught at Sesc, SP Escola de Teatro and Casa 11. Executive producer for Cia. Barbixas de Humor.

Maro Lesioti

Actress · director · improviser · educator — Athens

Works across theater, film and contemporary performance. Trained at Modern Times Drama School and LAMDA (London). Artistic director of the Impro Synthesis school (Athens) and co-artistic director of the I.TH.A.C.A. Festival. Her practice focuses on interdisciplinary processes and international collaborations across Europe and the United States.

Luana Proença

Actress · director · researcher · writer — Brasília

Holds a master's and a PhD in Theater. Trained at The Second City Hollywood. Creator of the Visando Impro project, director of the documentary Foi preciso ouvir outras vozes and author of books on the pedagogy of improvisation. Taught at Escola de Circo Chapitô (Lisbon) and has toured workshops across Europe, the United States, Asia and Australia.

03

International alliance

são paulo ↔ athens

Two cities. One mirrored process.

Our bond with Impro Synthesis, in Athens, takes shape in the creative partnership between Gabriel Caropreso and Maro Lesioti. Together they co-direct Eagerly Waiting (Aguardo Ansiosamente), an intercontinental show developed with simultaneous casts in Brazil and Greece.

The work investigates how affective relationships are built across concrete physical distance. Two actors communicate through a virtual space made of letters, audio messages and texts. Improvisation here does not serve comedy or quickness; it operates as a tool of relational dramaturgy.

Distance is both the theme and the condition of the creation. In the finale, the virtual device is destroyed on stage, breaking the mediation. The project generated an exchange tour with performances and courses in Barcelona, Cologne, Thessaloniki, Oslo and Uppsala.
